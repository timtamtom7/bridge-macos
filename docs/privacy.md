# Bridge Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-25**

## Overview

Bridge helps you manage and sync data between your iOS devices and Mac. Your privacy is our priority.

## Data Collection

Bridge processes the following data locally on your Mac:
- **Photos**: Transferred photos are stored in your specified location
- **Contacts**: Contact data synced from iOS devices
- **Messages**: Message history from iOS devices (when explicitly selected)
- **Device Information**: Device names, model numbers, storage capacity

**No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or transmitted to third parties.**

## Local Processing

All sync operations happen locally. Data from your iOS devices:
- Is processed on your Mac only
- May be stored in `~/Library/Application Support/Bridge/`
- Is encrypted before uploading to cloud storage (if enabled)

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Only non-sensitive preferences sync via your Apple ID
- Security scan results, photos, contacts, and messages **never** sync via iCloud
- Apple does not have access to your synced content

## Cloud Backup

When using cloud backup (Google Drive, Dropbox, S3):
- Data is encrypted with AES-256 before upload
- Cloud providers do not have access to unencrypted data
- You control your cloud account credentials

## Widgets

Widgets display cached sync status only. No personal data is shared with the widget extension.

## Third Parties

Bridge does not:
- Use analytics or tracking SDKs
- Display advertisements
- Share data with data brokers
- Include any third-party SDKs that collect data

## Children

Bridge is not directed at users under 13.
