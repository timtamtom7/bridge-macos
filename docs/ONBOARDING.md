# Bridge — Onboarding

4 screens introducing the iOS device bridge concept.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Bridge

**Headline:** Your iPhone, on your Mac.

**Body:** Bridge connects directly to your iOS device via USB — no cloud, no account needed. Manage photos, contacts, and backups right from your Mac.

**Illustration:** A stylized flat illustration of an iPhone and MacBook placed side by side, with a glowing cable connecting them. A subtle bridge icon spans the gap. Background in soft blue-gray gradient.

**CTA Button:** "Continue →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right, subtle)

---

## Screen 2 — Connect Your Device

**Headline:** Plug in to get started.

**Body:** Connect your iPhone or iPad to your Mac using a USB cable. Bridge will detect it automatically — make sure to tap "Trust" on your device when prompted.

**Illustration:** An illustration of a MacBook with a USB-C cable extending outward. The cable end shows a phone silhouette with a "Trust this computer?" prompt bubble. A green checkmark indicates a successful connection state. Clean, step-by-step visual.

**CTA Button:** "Check for Device"

**Secondary:** A small note: "Bridge uses a direct USB connection. Your data never leaves your Mac."

---

## Screen 3 — What You Can Do

**Headline:** Everything your device, nothing in the cloud.

**Body:** Three things, in a clean icon row:

- **Photos** — Browse and export photos and videos from your camera roll
- **Contacts** — View, edit, and sync contacts directly
- **Backup** — Create local backups without iCloud

**Illustration:** A three-panel illustration. Left panel: photo grid icon. Center panel: contact card icon. Right panel: external drive/backup icon. Each in a rounded card with the primary blue accent. Connected by a subtle dotted line to show they're all part of Bridge.

**CTA Button:** "Next →"

---

## Screen 4 — Ready to Bridge

**Headline:** You're all connected.

**Body:** Bridge is ready. Select a tab above to explore your device. Your data stays on your Mac — always.

**Illustration:** A celebratory but tasteful illustration: an iPhone and MacBook connected, with small spark icons around the connection point. A green "Connected" badge. No confetti — clean, like Apple.

**CTA Button:** "Start Using Bridge"
